Privacy Policy

MBriefX Daily Digest processes only the data necessary to generate a private daily summary from accounts I follow.

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties. Access tokens are stored securely on my machine/server and are used solely to read tweets via the X API.

The App does not collect end-user data beyond what is required by X OAuth for authentication. For removal or questions, please contact: youremail@example.com
